ABOUT
Welcome!
Hi, i am Swaminathan, a budding photographer. I started learning photography as hobby when i was pursueing my Engineering and got my first DSLR close to my graduation. Engineering student graduates whats most likely to happen? Ya happened to me too started working in MNC as Software Developer.
Intial years were hectic at work and couldnt do much in photography but things changed with time and i was able to continue my hobby.
I started clicking pictures of different ocassions within my family and simultaneosuly started to acquire more knowledge through blogs and You Tube. Soon i became 'the' person for my family members to get their most liked facebook profile pictures.
Increased confidence level created a craving in my mind to shoot a wedding without having other resposibilties. Thats when one of my best friends wedding was fixed. I went for the wedding and did the best i could and many of my pics went in to their marriage album though me not being primary photographer.
My love for photography has only increased since then and i have never missed photographing any occasion within my family/friends circle. If you feel my style syncs with your excitement about D-day/Birthday/Baby Shower photos then mail me about your occassion and together we can work to make it awesome.
Do not worry about the cost since i prefer experience rather than money. I will happily accept whatever you feel my photos are worthy.
